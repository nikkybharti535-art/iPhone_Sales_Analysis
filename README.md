# 📱 iPhone Sales Analysis

##  Objective
To analyze iPhone pricing, ratings, and customer behavior.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Plotly, Seaborn, Matplotlib

## 📊 Analysis Performed
- Price distribution analysis
- Price vs rating relationship
- Top reviewed products
- Correlation analysis
- Price segmentation (Low, Medium, High)

## 🔍 Key Insights
- No strong relationship between price and rating
- High-priced products do not guarantee better ratings
- Products with more reviews show stable ratings
- Apple follows a premium pricing strategy

##  Conclusion
Customer satisfaction depends more on product quality and brand value than price.

## ⚠️ Limitations
- Small dataset
- No proper discount data
- Data may not be up-to-date
